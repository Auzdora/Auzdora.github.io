About Me

Hey there, I’m Auzdora! When I’m not geeking out over algorithms and artificial intelligence, you’ll probably find me sketching, coding or exploring someplace new.

I’m a Computer Science and AI major with a serious thing for Distributed Systems, Databases, and AI. Sounds heavy, I know, but it’s super exciting for me. There’s just something about understanding how data works, how we can communicate more effectively, and figuring out all the brainy AI stuff that I can’t get enough of.

Now, you’re probably wondering where drawing and traveling fit into all this, right? Well, let me tell you, there’s nothing like sketching to let your creative juices flow, and nothing like a good trip to remember that the world is bigger than the size of your laptop screen! Plus, the change of pace and scenery give my brain a good reset, which always gets me ready to dive back into the tech world with a fresh pair of eyes.

This blog is where I post my tech notes and my understanding of different topics - it’s kind of my digital notebook. So, you’ll get a peek into my brain as I work through new concepts and ideas. And, who knows, you might just pick up a thing or two along the way!

I also share stories about my travels and maybe a sketch or two. So, whether you’re a tech junkie, an adventurous soul, or just someone who loves a good doodle, I think you’ll find something interesting here.

Stick around, join me on this techy journey, and let’s learn and grow together. You’re totally welcome to join in the conversation, so don’t be shy!
